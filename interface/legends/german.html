Problem: german credit

The goal is to decide the quality of creditability using the following variables (note: variables are normalized, some are categorical):

  • \( x_0= \) Balance of current account
  • \( x_1= \) Credit duration in months
  • \( x_2= \) Credit history quality
  • \( x_3= \) Purpose of credit
  • \( x_4= \) Credit amount (in German Marks)
  • \( x_5= \) Value of savings or stocks
  • \( x_6= \) Duration current employment
  • \( x_7= \) Instalment in % of available income
  • \( x_8= \) Marital Status / Sex
  • \( x_9= \) Further debtors / Guarantors
  • \( x_{10}= \) Duration of living in current household
  • \( x_{11}= \) Most valuable available assets
  • \( x_{12}= \) Age in years (categorized)
  • \( x_{13}= \) Further running credits
  • \( x_{14}= \) Flat type
  • \( x_{15}= \) Number of credits at this bank
  • \( x_{16}= \) Occupation type
  • \( x_{17}= \) Number of persons entitled to maintenance
  • \( x_{18}= \) Telephone is provided
  • \( x_{19}= \) Foreign worker
